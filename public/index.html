How It Works

A simple, secure process that transforms how trust flows through digital finance.

1

Bank Issues Credential

After completing standard KYC, your bank issues a Verifiable Credential using DIDgateway's open-source standard. This credential is cryptographically signed and belongs to you.

2

User Controls Identity

You hold your credential in a secure wallet. No centralized database stores your identity. You decide when and where to prove your compliance status.

3

Zero-Knowledge Verification

When accessing a DeFi protocol, you generate a zero-knowledge proof. This proves you're verified without revealing any personal data to the protocol.

4

Instant Access

DIDgateway's decentralized operator network validates your proof in real-time. The DeFi protocol grants access instantly ‚Äî no waiting, no repeated KYC.
